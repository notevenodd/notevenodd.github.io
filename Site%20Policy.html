1) This website does not create cookies with its domain (notevenodd.net)

2) I do not place advertisment from ad servers on this site (third party resources such as videos I embed may display ads, this is not my doing).

3) Browsing this website with Javascript deactivated should work quite well (unless a page states you need Javascript)

4) Those pages are hosted on GitHub. This is simpler for me and works very well. It means I do not have access to the webserver used to serve those pages (however GitHub does).

5) Because of my simple hosting setup, I cannot deploy self-hosted analytics (which would be the ideal solution) to check stats on this website traffic. Thus (like many websites) I do use Google Analytics. This may deposit some cookies from Google. Feel free to block that (If like this website, please do send me a message so I know that at least someone likes this blog).

6) On pages tagged as

Widgets

I load third-party Javascript from other sites (so called "social" media). This may load cookies from those sites. However I never link to those pages without a notice, so you can always skip those pages.

7) I may embed videos from video websites (primarily from YouTube but may also from Dailymotion or Vimeo)

8) Except from the three previous points above, I try not to hot/inline link to other sites' images, scripts, or resources. This thus should avoid leaking your referer information there.
